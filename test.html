Our Hstory

The Blouberg Annual Tournament was born in January 2025 with the successful hosting of our inaugural event. Recognising the positive impact it had on the community, local leaders unanimously agreed to transform this initiative into a monthly tournament. Since then, our mission has been to bring people together, celebrate local talent, and provide a platform for the youth to shine.

Our Purpose

At Blouberg Annual Tournament, we are driven by a clear purpose:

  • Promote and support local talent:We believe in the potential of every individual and strive to create opportunities for them to showcase their skills.
  • We believe in the potential of every individual and strive to create opportunities for them to showcase their skills.
  • Entertainment for the community: Our tournaments are not just competitions; they are celebrations of unity, joy, and community pride.
