Our Services

At Blouberg Tournament, we are dedicated to providing a range of services that go beyond just organizing football matches. Our mission is to foster community engagement, promote healthy lifestyles, and support those in need. Here are some of the key services we offer:

Soup kitchen for the poor

We run a soup kitchen during our tournaments to provide nutritious meals to those in need. Our goal is to ensure that everyone, regardless of their circumstances, has access to food and warmth.

Coaching service

Our coaching service is designed to help players of all ages and skill levels improve their game. We offer training sessions, workshops, and one-on-one coaching to help players reach their full potential on the field.

Gym service

We provide access to gym facilities and fitness programs to help players maintain peak physical condition. Our gym service includes strength training, cardio workouts, and flexibility exercises tailored to the needs of football players.

Social development service

With Stakeholder of local social development service we are committed to help in this regard We believe in the power of sports to bring about positive change in communities. Our social development service focuses on using football as a tool to promote teamwork, discipline, and leadership among youth.
