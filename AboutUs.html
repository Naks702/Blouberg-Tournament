About Us

Welcome to Blouberg Annual Tournament,

a non-profit communication organisation dedicated to fostering community spirit, nurturing local talent, and creating opportunities for the youth in our community.

Our Hstory

The Blouberg Annual Tournament was born in January 2025 with the successful hosting of our inaugural event. Recognising the positive impact it had on the community, local leaders unanimously agreed to transform this initiative into a monthly tournament. Since then, our mission has been to bring people together, celebrate local talent, and provide a platform for the youth to shine.

Our Mission and Vision

At Blouberg Annual Tournament, we believe that together, we can create a brighter future for our youth and a stronger, more united community. Whether you’re a participant, a sponsor, or a spectator, your support helps us achieve our goals. Join us in this journey of empowerment, entertainment, and community development. Together, let’s make every tournament a celebration of talent, unity, and hope.

Whom do we serve

Our efforts are focused on:

  1. Community members: Bringing people together to celebrate local talent and strengthen community bonds.
  2. Local business people: Partnering with businesses that share our vision of giving back to the community.
  3. Community clubs: Collaborating with local clubs to foster talent and encourage participation.
