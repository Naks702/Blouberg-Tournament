1. Organisation Overview

Organisation Name: Blouberg Annual Tournament

History:
Our organisation started in January 2025 by successfully hosting a tournament. Following the event, community leaders agreed that the tournament should be held monthly and that sponsorships should be sought to support it.

Mission and Vision:

Promote and support local talent

Provide positive activities to take kids off the streets

Offer entertainment for the community

Target Audience:

Community members

Local business owners

Community clubs

2. Website Goals and Objectives

Provide updates regarding the tournament

Raise brand awareness to attract sponsors

Increase awareness among community villages interested in participating
